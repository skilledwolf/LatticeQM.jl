Publications Using LatticeQM

The LatticeQM package was used in the context of the following publications and thesis:

  • Wolf, T. M. R.; Holst, M. F.; Sigrist, M.; Lado, J. L. (2022). Nonunitary multiorbital superconductivity from competing interactions in Dirac materials. Phys. Rev. Research 4, L012036. doi:10.1103/PhysRevResearch.4.L012036. https://link.aps.org/doi/10.1103/PhysRevResearch.4.L012036

  • Wolf, T. M. R.; Zilberberg, O.; Blatter, G.; Lado, J. L. (2021). Spontaneous valley spirals in magnetically encapsulated twisted bilayer graphene. Phys. Rev. Lett. 126, 056803. doi:10.1103/PhysRevLett.126.056803. https://link.aps.org/doi/10.1103/PhysRevLett.126.056803

  • Wolf, T. M. R. (2021). Electronic properties of twisted-layer graphene systems. PhD Thesis, ETH Zurich. doi:10.3929/ethz-b-000475934. https://doi.org/10.3929/ethz-b-000475934

  • Wolf, T. M. R.; Lado, J. L.; Blatter, G.; Zilberberg, O. (2019). Electrically Tunable Flat Bands and Magnetism in Twisted Bilayer Graphene. Phys. Rev. Lett. 123, 096802. doi:10.1103/PhysRevLett.123.096802. https://link.aps.org/doi/10.1103/PhysRevLett.123.096802
